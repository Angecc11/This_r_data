Chapter 1

1.1 big data overview

  • Huge volume
  • Complexity of data types and structures
  • Speed of new data creation and growth

Big data often described as having the 3 v’s - Volume - Variety - Velocity

What is driving the data deluge? - Mobile sensors - Social media - Video surveillance - Video rendering - Smart grids - Geophysical exploration - Medical imaging - Gene sequencing

Data structures

Most of big data is unstructured or semi structured.

  • Structured - spreadsheets, csv files etc
  • Semi-structures - XML files
  • “Quasi” structured - click stream data
  • Unstructured- text documents, PDF’s, videos, images
