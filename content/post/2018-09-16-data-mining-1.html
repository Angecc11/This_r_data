Data mining is the process of discovering patterns and structures in large datasets. It uses methods from statistics, computer science and machine learning.

It is more exploratory than multvariate analysis. The emphasis is on developing predictions from data.

Data mining has many practical applications

Business applications Help companies intelligently organize and nd information Credit scoring Predict purchasing behavior Recommend products/services Develop trading strategies Scientific applications Predict disease occurrence Find predictive data features Simplify high volume data

The Fréchet Bounds usually written as max{P(A) + P(B) − 1, 0} ≤ P(A ∩ B) ≤ min{P(A), P(B)}. The bounds are “tight” which means that there is no better lower or upper bound that holds generally.
